How it works

Three steps from install to every model.

01

Add a key — or don't

Paste in your OpenRouter or Gemini API key. Your key, your billing, your usage stays between you and the provider — GemGPT never sees the bill.

02

Or just open the app

No key on hand? GemGPT comes with a default key built in, so you're chatting the moment you install it. Add your own later, whenever you want more control.

03

Search and switch models

Browse every model your key unlocks — from quick, lightweight assistants to frontier‑level reasoning — and switch models mid‑conversation.

Features

One app, every facet of AI.

Bring your own key

Connect an OpenRouter or Gemini API key directly in the app. Nothing routes through a server you didn't choose.

Default key included

Skip setup entirely. GemGPT ships with a working key so first‑time users can start chatting in seconds.

Full model catalog

Search the complete list of models available through your key — not just a curated handful.

Choose your power level

Pick light, fast models for quick answers, or step up to the most capable models for harder problems — you decide what you spend.

Switch without losing context

Move between models mid‑conversation while keeping the thread intact, so you can compare answers side by side.

Multiple keys, one app

Store more than one key and flip between providers depending on the task in front of you.

Model spectrum

From quick replies to frontier reasoning.

Every key unlocks a full range of models. Light models answer fast and cost less per use. Frontier models take more time and budget, but handle the hardest reasoning. GemGPT lists them side by side so you always know what you're choosing.

Pricing and availability depend on your provider and key.
